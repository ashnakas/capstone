Core Technology

NanoVision Display Matrix

An ultra-thin, self-powered nanomaterial display embedded into AR contact lenses. Quantum dot optics and graphene micro-circuitry render high-resolution augmented overlays directly on the eye's surface — no external hardware, no external power.

// 01
Quantum Dot MicroLEDs

Self-emissive LEDs deliver high-resolution AR overlays while remaining fully transparent when inactive. Zero optical footprint at rest.

// 02
Graphene Circuitry

A near-invisible graphene layer serves as both display wiring and onboard processor. No external compute, no latency, no compromise.

// 03
Bio-Nano Power Cells

Tear fluid bioelectricity and thin-film solar harvesting power the lens continuously. The human eye becomes the battery.

// 04
Neural Interface Chip

Micro-sensors read eye movement and surface biosignals to drive AR interactions — no external controller, no gesture device, no delay.

// 05

An ultra-compact high-bandwidth antenna streams reality in real time. Sub-millisecond latency. Your worldview, always current.
